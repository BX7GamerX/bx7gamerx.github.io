About Me

Hello, I'm Sir Simon, a passionate Computer Science enthusiast, a programming geek, and a creative mind with a diverse set of interests ranging from aviation to gaming and even pencil art. I’m constantly evolving, learning, and exploring new technologies, and my goal is to make meaningful contributions to the world of technology, whether it’s through innovative backend development, AI, or blockchain projects.

My Journey in Technology

I am currently expanding my skills in C++, Python, Rust, and web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’m particularly intrigued by the intersection of AI and blockchain and how they can be leveraged to build decentralized systems that are more efficient and secure.

In my recent projects, I’ve worked on GainChain, a decentralized social media platform on the Internet Computer (ICP) blockchain, where I contribute as a backend developer. In this project, I’ve also implemented a recommendation system using a C++ neural network that analyzes social media content and recommends relevant posts. This neural network is integrated into the backend architecture, which aims to offer users a more personalized experience.

Another project close to my heart is SpadeChain, a tool and infrastructure platform I’m building for blockchain and AI developers. It's designed to simplify the development of decentralized applications (dApps) and AI models, providing essential resources for creators in these fields.

My Creative Side

When I’m not coding or thinking about system architecture, I love to immerse myself in aviation—an industry that has always fascinated me. I enjoy reading about the latest technological advancements in aircraft and the aviation sector. I’m also a gamer, finding enjoyment in exploring virtual worlds and tackling complex challenges.

I’ve also dabbled in pencil art, creating sketches and designs that express my creativity. Whether it’s drawing people, animals, or abstract concepts, art allows me to channel my imagination in a different way and brings balance to my tech-heavy routine.

Current Focus

At the moment, I’m focused on refining my full-stack development skills and am working on a portfolio website where I showcase my skills, projects, and creative pursuits. This website serves as both a digital portfolio and an ongoing project where I implement the latest trends in web development. I aim to blend minimalism and user-centric design to create an intuitive, elegant interface.

In the future, I hope to continue building scalable AI models and blockchain applications while also exploring new realms of 3D animation and virtual reality.
